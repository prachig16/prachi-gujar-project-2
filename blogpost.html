shoes around the world

Monday May 4th, 2019 by Dieula

You can only go forward by making mistakes. Clothes mean nothing until someone lives in them. I can design a collection in a day and I always do, cause I've always got a load of Italians on my back, moaning that it's late. Never in my wildest dreams did I entertain the idea that I would become a fashion designer. A lot of self-importance goes on in the fashion industry. I'm not like that

You can hide so much behind theatrics, and I don't need to do that any more. The key to my collections is sensuality. I've always thought of the T-shirt as the Alpha and Omega of the fashion alphabet. The great thing about fashion is that it always looks forward. I am no longer concerned with sensation and innovation, but with the perfection of my style.

You have to stay true to your heritage; that's what your brand is about. I can't get sucked into that celebrity thing, because I think it's just crass. The only way to do something in depth is to work hard. Success isn't about the end result, it's about what you learn along the way. I like the body. I like to design everything to do with the body.
